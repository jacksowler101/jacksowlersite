Welcome, and thanks for stopping by.

I'm Jack. By day I work in energy modelling — trying to understand how the power system
behaves and how it pays for itself. But I'm curious about a lot more than electricity markets,
and this site is where I follow those threads in public.

You'll find two kinds of thing here. **Writing** is where I work through ideas in prose —
energy and climate mostly, but also AI, policy, a bit of science, and whatever else has caught
my attention. **Projects** are the things you can actually click on: interactive tools and data
visualisations, some finished, some half-built, some still just ideas I want to get to.

That interactive bent is the whole point. Where I can, I'd rather hand you something to explore
than show you a static chart and tell you what to think. The
[GB generation mix explorer](/projects/gb-generation-mix/) is a small first example — have a
play with it.

What's next? Deep-dives on **TNUoS** network charging and the **balancing market**, a tracker
for the UK's climate targets, an experiment forecasting electricity demand with machine
learning — and, when I need a break from the grid, the odd sport project. More soon.
