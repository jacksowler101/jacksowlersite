Expected goals (xG) has quietly become the most useful number in football — a measure of chance
quality that cuts through the noise of who actually scored on the day.

**The idea:** an interactive explorer for Premier League xG — over- and under-performance by team
and season, shot maps, and "who got lucky" tables. A break from the grid, and a good excuse to
play with a different dataset.

Strictly for fun. On the roadmap.
