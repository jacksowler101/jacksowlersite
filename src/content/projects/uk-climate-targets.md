The UK has legally binding carbon budgets and a 2050 net-zero target — but progress varies
enormously by sector, and the headline numbers hide a lot.

**The idea:** a clean, interactive dashboard that tracks emissions against the budgets over
time, lets you break the trajectory down by sector (power, transport, buildings, industry,
agriculture), and makes it obvious where we're on track and where we're not.

This one is on the roadmap — an idea I want to build next.
