A self-contained dashboard explaining how Great Britain's key power-market mechanisms work and
what the data says. Each topic covers the context, why government built it, how the mechanism
works, and interactive charts — plus a market-wide "revenue stack" infographic showing which
technologies can earn from which revenue streams, ordered by proximity to delivery.

Built with verified figures from DESNZ, NESO, Ofgem and LCCC.
