Transmission Network Use of System (TNUoS) charges are one of the strongest — and least
understood — signals deciding *where* new generation gets built in Great Britain. They are also
being reformed, which could move a lot of money around the map.

**What I'm building:** an interactive GB map and tariff explorer that lets you see how zonal
generation and demand charges look today, what the proposed reforms would do to them, and what
that means for the economics of building in the north versus the south.

Status: in progress — I'm pulling the charging data together and sketching the map. Check back
soon.
